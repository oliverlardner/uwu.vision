02 The Studio

Spartans! What is your profession?

Immersive work requires a completely new depth of technical understanding — and we are not just another video house.

We have a VR video game studio attached. That gives us a unique and thorough understanding of what users face in highly dynamic VR environments, and how to design for them. Not only do we have decades of experience in stereoscopy, but also direction, capture, stitching, mastering, and UX. Nobody else brings all this experience together (and if they do we'd love to meet them!).

If you need custom interactive or custom playback software, we've got you. Then again, if you don't, wouldn't you like to know that too?
